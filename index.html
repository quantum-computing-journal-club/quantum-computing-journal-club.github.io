Welcome

This is a journal club that seeks to learn and discuss modern advancements in the field of quantum computing, both from a theoretical stand point than from a more experimental point of view.

The goal of this club is to explore new computational paradigms, new implementations ...; depending on the topic prior knowledge might or might not be expected, for more see Background Material

Organized by Giovanni Cemin .
